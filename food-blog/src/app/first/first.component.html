1 : Hot take: I don’t peel them! Apple peels are delicious. I just cut the apples into quarters or chunks and toss ’em in the Instant Pot.

2 : I do two cinnamon sticks (or, in this case, some shards of cinnamon stick left at the bottom of the bag) and 1/2 cup water..

3: I cook the applesauce for 5 minutes – then it’s a quick hybrid mash/blend with the immersion blender.

4 : This is just such a delight in our household. Eaten warm, cold, with a meal, or on its own. Simplicity for the win!
